About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the Australian National University, Solomon Lab, focusing on plant–microbe interactions, heavy metal stress, and plant stress physiology. My PhD research focuses on understanding the mechanistic basis of the interaction between the fungal pathogen Zymoseptoria tritici and wheat. I aspire to evolve from an enthusiastic researcher to a skilled scientist in plant science.

Research Interests

  • Plant Stress Physiology
