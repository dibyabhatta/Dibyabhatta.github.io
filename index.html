About Me

I am a PhD candidate at Solomon Lab, Australian National University. My PhD research focuses on understanding the mechanistic basis of the interaction between the fungal pathogen Zymoseptoria tritici and wheat. I aspire to evolve from an enthusiastic researcher to a skilled scientist in plant science.

On my YouTube channel, I run the "Candid with Candidates" series with PhD friends around the globe: youtube.com/agri_dibu
