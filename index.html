About Me

I am a PhD candidate at Solomon Lab in the Australian National University. My PhD research focuses on understanding the mechanistic basis of the interaction between the fungal pathogen Zymoseptoria tritici and wheat. I aspire to evolve from an enthusiastic researcher to a skilled scientist in plant science.
